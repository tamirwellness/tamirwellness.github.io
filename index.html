Location

We are located at Terelj National Park, one of the top tourist destinations and the third biggest protected area in Mongolia.

Only one hour drive (50km) on a paved road from the capital city Ulaanbaatar.

Restaurant

Tamir restaurant is capable of serving 100 people at the same time. We serve breakfast, lunch, and dinner that include both international and domestic dishes.

Gers

We have 26 gers (Mongolian traditional yurt) with a total capacity of 100 people.

Mini bar

There is a mini bar inside the restaurant, where we have a fine selection of hot and cold beverages.

Ger Interior

Our gers are all handmade. Traditionally hand painted in colorful patterns expressing an authentic feel.

A cosy and comfortable ger from which you can look up the skylight and enjoy a night under the stars!

Fantastic Rocks

A mini hiking tour to explore over 30 incredible rock formations. The trip takes approximately 2 hours.

Beautiful panoramic view from the top of the mountain, green meadow with colorful flowers, huge rock formations and interesting hiking paths are all there waiting for you.

Visiting Nomadic Family

Terelj National Park is home to nomadic Mongolians, offering you a chance to interact with the authentic people of Mongolia – hospitable, gracious and welcoming.

Visit real Mongolian nomadic families and experience their everyday life, custom, and tradition.

Cuisine

You can taste Mongolian traditional meals such as khuushuur, buuz, tsuivan and khorkhog in our restaurant.

Seminar

We provide a room for events or meetings with a capacity of up to 50 people. Coffee, tea, and soft snacks can be served during the breaks.

Horse Riding

Mongolia is the best destination for horse-riding trip, and especially, Terelj National Park is the best recommended place for a horseback travel.

We offer horseback riding trip in the beautiful horse trail region with wild flower steppes, rivers, and mountains. You can rent horses hourly or daily basis with a guide.
